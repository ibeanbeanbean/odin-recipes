Coffee

Coffee is coffee ya know. Ahh lovely, a mug of hot... java.

Ingredients

  • Cawffee beans or grounds, depending on your level of snobbery
  • A nice mug

Optional

  • Milk, because you don't want to be a sick, sick, espresso drinker and have some self respect
  • Syrups, to taste something other than bitterness and regret
  • Chocolate dusting, because you're just a cute little cappucino enjoyer

Steps

  1. Grind the beans or put the grains into the Portafilter (Yes, I'm assuming you have an actual coffee machine and are not an instant coffee consuming sub-human scum)
  2. Wait until the temperature light, lights up
  3. Press the button
  4. Stop after a bit
  5. Press the steaming wand button and wait for it to light up
  6. Steam the shit out of that milk, frothy, not frothy, spraying all over your kitchen? go mental!
  7. Put some sizzurp in there and shake a light chocolate dusting on top, preferably with a stencil of a happy face, or a cat, to improve taste
  8. Sip but keep your mouth closed, no one wants to hear you slurping ok
