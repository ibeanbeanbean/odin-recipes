Cereal

Cereal is incredible, you can mix and match all sorts of ingredients, bringing them all together with milk to create an amalgam of your own creation.

Ingredients

  • Cereal
  • Milk

Optional

  • Fruit
  • Syrups/Sauces

Steps

  1. Get a bowl
  2. Pour your cereal of choice. (important)
  3. Pour the milk of your choosing into the bowl.
  4. Sprinkle any extras you feel like on top.
  5. Depending on your cereal, eat immediately to avoid sogginess, or allow a few minutes for the milk to soak in. You do you.
  6. Slurp it up.
